to solve a rubic 3*3 cube

first step:

solve the white cross

Find the yellow center piece

  1. Create a white cross on the top face by positioning white edge pieces so that the other color

  2. on each edge piece matches the center pieces on the adjacent faces.


second step:

Solve the White Corners

  1. Place white corner pieces so that they complete the white face while aligning with the adjacent center colors.

  2. Use algorithms like R U R' U' to position and orient the corner pieces.


third step:

Solve the Middle Layer Edges

  1. Flip the cube so the white face is on the bottom.

  2. Position the edge pieces in the middle layer to match the center colors on the adjacent sides.

  3. Use the algorithms U R U' R' U' F' U F or U' L' U L U F U' F' to move the edges to the correct position.


fourth step:

Solve the Yellow Cross

  1. Focus on the top (yellow) face.

  2. Create a yellow cross on the top face using algorithms like F R U R' U' F' to correctly orient the yellow edges.


step number five:

Position the Yellow Corners


  1. Position the yellow corners (they don't have to be correctly oriented yet).

  2. Use the algorithm U R U' L' U R' U' L to position them.
